TruckMatch is a platform that connects construction companies with verified truck operator companies, making it easy to find, book, and manage trucks and heavy equipment for construction projects.

Construction companies post a job with their requirements. Qualified truck operator companies receive the request, submit their pricing, and once accepted, assign the right truck and driver for the job.

TruckMatch supports a wide range of equipment including Tipper Trucks, Water Trucks, Flatbeds, Tilt Trays, Hiab Cranes, Concrete Pumps, Vacuum Trucks, Machinery Transport, and many more.
